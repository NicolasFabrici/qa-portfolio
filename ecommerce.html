E-Commerce Platform

Quality Case Study – Risk-Based Testing Approach

Executive Summary

This project involved defining and executing a comprehensive testing strategy for a revenue-critical e-commerce platform. The main objective was to ensure transactional integrity, accurate price calculations and secure payment processing.

A risk-based testing approach was implemented to prioritize high-impact areas such as checkout and payment gateway integration.

Business Context

Web application supporting product browsing, user authentication, cart management and online payments. The system handles high daily traffic and processes sensitive financial transactions.

Scope

  • User authentication and session management
  • Product filtering and search
  • Cart price calculation logic
  • Checkout process
  • Payment validation and order confirmation

Risk Analysis

Critical Risk: Payment failure during transaction
Impact: Revenue loss and customer dissatisfaction
Mitigation: Early validation and regression focus on payment scenarios
High Risk: Incorrect cart total calculation
Impact: Financial discrepancy
Mitigation: Boundary value and price validation tests

Testing Strategy

  • Smoke testing before each release candidate
  • Full functional regression cycle
  • Boundary value analysis for quantity and price fields
  • Negative testing for payment scenarios
  • Exploratory testing focused on checkout usability

Results & Impact

Early detection of payment validation defects prevented potential revenue loss before production release.

The structured regression approach significantly reduced post-release issues and increased stakeholder confidence.

Lessons Learned

  • Prioritizing business-critical flows reduces production risk.
  • Clear severity classification improves resolution time.
  • Risk alignment must drive test coverage decisions.
